Pain

Nagato was a shinobi of the Hidden Rain Village. Having formed Akatsuki together with his friends, the same orphans of war, Nagato dreamed of bringing peace to the cruel world of shinobi. However, after Yahiko's death, Nagato adopted the pseudonym Pain and partnered with Konan to become the leader of the new Akatsuki.

Konan

A kunoichi of Hidden Rain, and one of the founders of the first Akatsuki organization. After Yahiko's death, she teamed up with Nagato, who later took control of Akatsuki.

Itachi

Itachi Uchiha renegade shinobi, former member of the Anbu, was the genius of the Uchiha clan from the Hidden Leaf. Itachi became an international criminal, destroying his entire clan.

Kisame

Kisame Hoshigaki was a shinobi of the Hoshigaki Clan from the Hidden Mist. After joining the Seven Ninja Swordsmen of the Mist, he became renegade ninja and was later assigned to Itachi as a member of the Akatsuki.

Sasori

Sasori was an S-rank renegade ninja of the Puppeteer Squad of Hidden Sand Village and a member of Akatsuki, where he was a partner of Orochimaru and, later, Deidara.

Deidara

Deidara was an S-grade renegade ninja from the Hidden Stone. After leaving the village, Deidara was forced to join Akatsuki, and became its youngest member.

Kakuzu

Kakuzu was an S-rank renegade ninja from Hidden Falls, as well as a member of Akatsuki who worked in tandem with Hidan.

Hidan

Hidan is an S-rank renegade ninja from Hidden Hot Springs who later joined Akatsuki. He was Kakuzu's partner despite their mutual dislike for each other.

Orachimaru

Orachimaru is one of the legendary Sannin of the Hidden Leaf Village. After leaving Konoha, Orochimaru soon joined the Akatsuki elite criminal organization.

Juzo Biwa

Juzo was a Hidden Mist jоуnin and a member of the Seven Ninja Swordsmen of the Mist. He was world famous for his brutality. Juzo preferred to approach missions as professionally as possible, so he specially designed combat formations with Itachi.

Zetsu

Zetsu was a half member of Akatsuki Zetsu along with Black Zetsu. Unlike Black Zetsu, White Zetsu, nicknamed "Chatterbox", was lighthearted and playful with a habit of teasing others.

Tobi

Obito Uchiha was a shinobi from the Uchiha clan of Konohagakure and a member of Team Minato. It was believed that he died during the Third Shinobi World War. In fact, Obito saved from death and trained Madara Uchiha.
